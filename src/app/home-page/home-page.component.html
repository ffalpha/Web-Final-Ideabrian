What Is Ideabrain

Why you should use it

IdeaBrain builds solution teams from a secure network of people where it leads to solutions and insights for the arising problems. By gathering and utilizing the Sri Lankan's brilliant minds, positive innovations it opens a path with passionate to be benefited in a way of development of Sri Lanka. We intend to achieve this through following objectives.

What will you get

This is a digitized platform containing three sub platforms. Using this platform the anyone can submit any ongoing problem and get the help to find the solutions from the community of the users on the platform. If the solutions are good enough and easy to process they can make it happen without waiting for the support of the government.
